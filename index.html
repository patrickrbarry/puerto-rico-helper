Puerto Rico Opening Helper

2-player official variant – early-round advisor

Who are you this round?

Official 2-player start:
– Governor: Indigo + 3 doubloons
– Second player: Corn + 2 doubloons
The helper uses this to assign starting plantations; you can still adjust doubloons below if they change.

In 2-player, you alternate picking until each player has chosen 3 roles (6 total), leaving 1 unused role with a coin. The pick number is just “how early or late in the round is this choice?”

Face-up Plantations

2-player = 3 face-up tiles. Leave “None” where there is no tile.
