2-player official variant – early-round advisor

Who are you this round?

Official 2-player start:
– Governor: Indigo + 3 doubloons
– Second player: Corn + 2 doubloons
The helper uses this to assign starting plantations; you can still adjust doubloons below if they change.

In 2-player, you alternate picking until each player has chosen 3 roles (6 total), leaving 1 unused role with a coin. If you are Governor, your picks are 1, 3, 5. If the opponent is Governor, your picks are 2, 4, 6.

Face-up Plantations

2-player = 3 face-up tiles. When you (or your opponent) take one, update this row to match the board.

Settler may always choose a quarry (while any remain), which is often important early.

Available Roles

Check the roles that are still available when it’s your turn to pick. (2-player: 7 total roles, 6 are chosen, 1 is left over with a coin.) When you tap a recommendation or apply the opponent’s last pick, the corresponding role will be removed here.

Opponent’s Last Pick

Set the role and, if relevant, what they did with it. Then click “Apply opponent move” to update their engine, remove the role from availability, and update the board.

Recommendations
