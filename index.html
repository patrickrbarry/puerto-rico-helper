Puerto Rico Opening Helper

2-player official variant – Round 1 focus

2-Player Setup (fixed)

Official 2-player variant: Governor starts with Indigo + 3 doubloons, second player with Corn + 2 doubloons. We hard-code the plantations and you can adjust doubloons if they change mid-round.

In the official 2-player rules, each round both players alternate choosing roles until each has taken 3 (6 total), leaving 1 unused role with a coin. This selector is just telling the helper how early/late in the round you are.

Face-up Plantations

2-player = 3 face-up tiles. Leave any unused slot as “None”.

Available Roles

Check the roles that are still available when it’s your turn to pick. (Official 2-player: 7 total roles, 6 chosen each round, 1 left over.)

Opponent’s Last Pick (optional)

This doesn’t affect the math much yet but gives context and will matter more as we refine the engine.
